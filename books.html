歩いても 歩いても/步履不停/Still Walking

I am a fan of Chinese and Japanese books (translated into Chinese). This is a book from Hirokazu Koreeda that I really like. I also recomend the movie. I read it in the translated Chinese version since I don't really know Japanese. Very touching book and movie about family relationship.
